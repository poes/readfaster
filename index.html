Read Faster!

Research shows that you read faster if your eyes stay at the same point while reading. I've seen videos that demonstrate this idea and it worked pretty well. If you want to test this yourself, please do! Press "start" when you start reading the text and "stop" when you are done. You will be given a time in seconds which you can compare with the next test.
